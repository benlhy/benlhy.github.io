Introducing the ULCEK

The Ultra Low Cost Electronics Kit is built on the idea of modularity. The kit provides the essentials you need to start having fun. Once you learn the basics, you are free to branch out to specific areas you are interested in.


At the center of this kit are two time-tested microprocessors: the ATTINY85 and ESP8266. The ATTINY85 is a favourite for microcontroller programmming, while the ESP8266 is a mainstay of Internet Of Things projects. Both the suggested boards for these chips are programmed with the Arduino IDE, through either USB or a microUSB.

Real World

In this kit you are using the same parts and materials that real world engineers use to prototype their designs. Ease of learning is provided in the tutorials, not the components. Your skills and knowledge can transfer immediately to other projects

Self Sourcing

All details on what to buy and where to buy the parts are provided. The price of $10 is calculated on the premise of assembling just one kit. Buy it from us, or build it yourself: you get the hardware, we'll handle the tutorials
